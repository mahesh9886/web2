One Solution to your Farm, Sat2Farm

A satellite-based agricultural smart phone application.

My Farm mobile application, a satellite-based agricultural smart phone application, is mainly developed to help the users for understanding their farm health, providing irrigation advisory as well as dealing with weather forecast. It is a simple application wherein the user plots his/her farm boundary and the requirements about the farm are provided through the same.

How does it work

Big Data

With Massive Earth Observation Data, Coupled With Recent Advancements In Machine Learning, Have Helped Us Develop Unique Solution To Leverage Them In Agriculture, Insurance And A Wide Variety Of Sectors.

Weather Forecasting

We Are Providing Next 14days Weather Report.

Machine Learning

With Massive Earth Observation Data, Coupled With Recent Advancements In Machine Learning, Have Helped Us To Develop Unique Solution To Leverage Them In Agriculture, Insurance And A Wide Variety Of Sectors.

Physical Modelling

Additionally Understanding The Water Content In The Soil Is Important For Farmers To Optimize The Usage Of Water Over Their Farmland. Our In-House Built Physical Modelling Algorithms, Predict Soil Moisture In All Weather Conditions.

Predictive Analytics

To Better Understand The Risk Involved Over A Farm, We Simulate Flood And Drought Risk Scenarios By Leveraging Historical Events, Coupled With Future Climate Change Scenarios, To Predict Credit Score Of The Farm, Aiding Banks To Make Informed Decision On Load Approvals.
