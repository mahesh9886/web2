B2B Sales Executive

  • Home
  • Careers
Satyukt Analytics Pvt Ltd

Satyukt Analytics Pvt Ltd, a Bengaluru based company is an expert in Microwave remote sensing. At Satyukt Analytics we believe in equal opportunity and we have high regards towards innovative talents. We are a team of strong dedicated people. Job openings at Satyukt

The ideal candidate will have strong creative skills and A suitable candidate for this job profile should entail the expertise and zeal in lead generation as well as completing the sales funnel cycle by converting the potential leads into deals.

Essential Knowledge, Skills, and Experience
  • At least 2 years of experience in B2B sales
  • Experience of working in agriculture input, output or BFSI sector.
  • Expertise in using a CRM, sales tools, and LinkedIn.
  • Excellence in sales and account management.
  • MBA/PGDM in sales or related degree
Preferred Experience
  • Desire to work in a fast-paced environment with high growth.
  • Highly Organized and skilled in time management and prioritizing ability.
  • An entrepreneurial spirit.
  • Experience of working in a start-up environment.
  • Sense of responsibility in task completion and dependability.
  • Team leader.
  • Proficient in communication, negotiation skills.
  • Creative personality.
Roles and Responsibilites
  • Perform lead generation and other customer acquisition activities.
  • Manage contact database (CRM).
  • Maintain effective follow up and closing percentages for inbound/outbound lead funnel provided.
  • Organize data, clean up pipeline, and produce weekly reporting
  • Actively involved in writing technical proposals as and when required to be shared with the prospects/clients including agri-input, agri-output, corporates, FPO, government agencies/organizations and or Non profit organizations.
  • Proficient in presenting and giving demos of the existing products based on analysis including gathering data, identifying and supporting solution development, and conducting business analyses with effective project management.
  • Able to properly demonstrate the features and benefits of the product of the company and keep the lead engaged in the preferred mode/format for sales conversions.
  • Coordinate with the product development team in terms of improvisation of existing product and or developing new features/complete product based on the feedback received after the demo with the client/prospect.
  • Proactively identify and implement new strategies to improve the sales cycle.
  • Creating creative outbound prospecting strategy and executing on that strategy to break through to busy prospects: cold call, email, social selling, direct mail, etc.
  • Develop external partnerships (e.g., startups, non-profits, non-governmental-organizations, government agencies, corporates etc.) to support the development and scaling of technical solutions.
  • Tracking the changes in market dynamics and implementing amendments in various product offerings based on market, competition and customer’s requirements.
  • Work with executive leadership to define, launch, and drive strategic sustainability initiatives spanning the organization.
  • Establish analytics best practices and ensure high quality deliverables.
  • Achieving and exceeding weekly/monthly performance metrics (appointments set, qualified leads worked, deals closed, etc.)
  • Enable critical decision making by aligning and working with cross-functional stakeholders.
